What is Liposuction?

Liposuction is a surgical procedure that removes localized fat deposits to sculpt and contour the body.

It is not a weight loss procedure, but rather a body contouring technique designed to improve shape and proportion.

  • Targets stubborn fat areas
  • Improves body contour
  • Enhances natural proportions

Is Liposuction Right for You?

  • You have stubborn fat that does not improve with exercise
  • You are close to your ideal body weight
  • You want more defined body contours
  • You have good skin elasticity

Liposuction vs Tummy Tuck

Liposuction removes fat but does not address loose skin or muscle separation. A tummy tuck is more suitable when there is excess skin or weakened abdominal muscles.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is liposuction painful?
The procedure is performed under anaesthesia, so you will not feel pain during surgery. Mild discomfort may occur during recovery.
How long do results last?
Results are long-lasting if you maintain a stable weight and healthy lifestyle.
Am I a good candidate?
Ideal candidates are those with stubborn fat deposits and good skin elasticity.
What is the recovery time?
Most patients return to light activities within a few days, with full recovery over several weeks.
Are there visible scars?
Incisions are small and strategically placed, resulting in minimal visible scarring.
