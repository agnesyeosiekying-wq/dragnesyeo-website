Breast Augmentation

Enhancing shape and volume with a focus on natural, balanced results tailored to your body.

Is Breast Augmentation Right for Me?

Many patients seek breast augmentation to improve volume, restore shape after pregnancy, or achieve better proportion with their body. The goal is not to look artificial, but to enhance your natural silhouette.

My Approach

I focus on achieving results that are natural, balanced, and suited to your individual anatomy. Implant selection, size, and technique are carefully chosen to ensure long-term safety and aesthetic harmony.

What to Expect

The procedure is performed under appropriate anaesthesia, with careful planning to minimise scarring and optimise recovery. Most patients are able to return to light activities within a short period, with full recovery over several weeks.

Safety & Considerations

All surgical procedures carry risks. A thorough consultation is important to understand your goals, medical history, and suitability for surgery.

Consultation

Every patient is different. A personalised consultation allows us to discuss your expectations and determine the most suitable approach for you.
