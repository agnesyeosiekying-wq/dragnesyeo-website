Breast Augmentation

Enhancing volume and shape with a focus on natural, proportionate results.

Common Concerns

• Will it look unnatural?
• Will it feel too obvious?
• What is the recovery like?

My Approach

The goal is not to create an artificial look, but to enhance your natural proportions. Careful selection of implant size, shape and placement ensures results that are balanced and harmonious.

Benefits

• Improved body proportion
• Enhanced confidence
• Natural-looking volume
• Long-lasting results

Procedure Overview

The surgery is performed under appropriate anaesthesia. Incisions are carefully planned to minimise visible scarring. Most patients can return to light activities within 1–2 weeks.
