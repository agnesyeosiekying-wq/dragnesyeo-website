The Procedure

What to Expect

01 — Consultation
A detailed discussion to understand your goals. Implant size and placement are planned to suit your anatomy.

02 — Surgery
Performed under general anaesthesia. Implants are placed through carefully selected incision sites.

03 — Recovery
Most return to light activities in a few days. Swelling subsides over weeks for natural results.

Understanding the Techniques

Breast Implants

Silicone implants provide a reliable and predictable way to enhance breast volume and projection based on your body proportions.

Fat Grafting

Fat is gently harvested and processed to enhance breast shape. This technique offers a softer, more natural enhancement.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the recovery period?

Most patients can return to sedentary work within 5–7 days. Strenuous exercise and heavy lifting should be avoided for at least 4–6 weeks to ensure proper healing.

Will the results look natural?

Dr. Agnes focuses on achieving harmony rather than just "perfection". By selecting the right implant profile or using fat grafting, the goal is a silhouette that feels natural to your unique body.

Do breast implants need to be replaced?

Breast implants are not considered lifetime devices. While they are highly durable, they may need to be replaced or removed in the future due to normal wear or changes in personal preference.

Where will the incisions be located?

Incision sites are carefully selected during consultation to minimize visible scarring. Common options include the crease under the breast (inframammary) or around the nipple (periareolar).
