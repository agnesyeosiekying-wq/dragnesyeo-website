What is a Tummy Tuck?

A tummy tuck (abdominoplasty) is a surgical procedure that removes excess skin and fat while tightening weakened abdominal muscles.

  • Removes loose skin after weight loss or pregnancy
  • Tightens abdominal muscles
  • Improves waist contour

Who is it for?

  • Loose abdominal skin
  • Post-pregnancy changes
  • Separated abdominal muscles
  • Stable body weight

Types of Tummy Tuck

  • Mini Tummy Tuck – For lower abdomen only
  • Full Tummy Tuck – Addresses entire abdomen
  • Extended Tummy Tuck – Includes flanks and sides

Benefits

  • Flatter abdomen
  • Improved posture
  • Better body proportion

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between tummy tuck and liposuction?
Tummy tuck removes skin and tightens muscles, while liposuction removes fat only.
How long is recovery?
Most patients recover within a few weeks.
