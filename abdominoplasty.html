What is Abdominoplasty?

Abdominoplasty, commonly known as a tummy tuck, removes excess skin and fat from the abdominal area while tightening weakened muscles. This procedure creates a smoother, firmer abdominal profile and is ideal for patients whose contour has not improved with diet or exercise alone.

  • Loose abdominal skin

  • Stable body weight

Procedure Options

Mini Tummy Tuck

Targets the lower abdomen with a smaller incision. Suitable for mild skin laxity.

  • Shorter recovery
  • Smaller incision
  • Less invasive

Full Tummy Tuck

Addresses the entire abdomen with muscle tightening and skin removal.

  • Full correction
  • Muscle repair
  • Long-lasting results

Extended Tummy Tuck

Includes flanks and sides for more comprehensive body contouring.

  • 360° contouring
  • Flank tightening
  • Dramatic results

Common Questions

Am I a good candidate?
Ideal candidates have excess skin, weakened abdominal muscles, and are close to their ideal body weight with stable health.
How long is recovery?
Most patients return to light activities within 2 weeks, with full recovery over several weeks and final results in a few months.
Will there be visible scarring?
Scars are placed low on the abdomen and fade over time. Proper care helps achieve optimal healing.
Can it be combined with other procedures?
Yes, tummy tuck is often combined with liposuction or breast procedures depending on your goals.
How long do results last?
Results are long-lasting if you maintain a stable weight and healthy lifestyle.
