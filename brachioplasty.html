What is Brachioplasty?

Brachioplasty, commonly known as an arm lift, removes excess skin and fat from the upper arms while tightening underlying tissue. This procedure creates a smoother, more toned appearance, and more youthful-looking arms - ideal for patients experiencing sagging or loose skin that won't respond to exercise alone.

Key Benefits

Sleek Arm Contour

Achieve toned, natural-looking arms that complement your body proportions.

Excess Skin Removal

Eliminate loose, sagging skin caused by aging, weight loss, or reduced elasticity.

Improved Clothing Fit

Enjoy better fit in dresses, tops, and fitted clothing without excess arm bulk.

Who is it for?

  • Loose or sagging upper arm skin
  • Post weight loss patients
  • Reduced skin elasticity

Procedure Options

Limited Incision

Smaller incision placed along the inner arm for mild to moderate skin laxity. Ideal for patients with minimal excess skin.

  • Minimal scarring
  • Shorter recovery
  • Local anaesthesia option

Traditional Brachioplasty

Standard arm lift with incision from armpit to elbow. Addresses significant skin excess and improves overall arm contour.

  • Comprehensive reshaping
  • Effective skin removal
  • Long-lasting results

Extended Brachioplasty

Extends beyond the upper arm to include side chest or back area for more complete contouring.

  • Full arm contouring
  • Side and back tightening
  • Transformative results

Not sure which option suits you? A personalised consultation will help determine the most appropriate approach.

Common Questions

Am I a good candidate?
Ideal candidates have loose or sagging upper arm skin, good overall health, and stable body weight. A consultation will determine the most suitable approach for you.
How long is recovery?
Most patients return to light activities within 1 week. Swelling gradually improves, with final results visible over 6–8 weeks.
Will there be visible scarring?
Incisions are placed along the inner arm where they are less noticeable. Scars typically fade over time and become less visible with proper care.
Can it be combined with other procedures?
Yes, brachioplasty can be combined with procedures such as liposuction or body contouring to achieve more comprehensive results.
How long do results last?
Results are long-lasting when maintaining a stable weight and healthy lifestyle. Natural aging will continue but improvements are sustained.
