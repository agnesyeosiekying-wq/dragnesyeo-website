What is Brachioplasty?

Brachioplasty, commonly known as an arm lift, removes excess skin and fat from the upper arms to create a smoother, more toned appearance.

Key Benefits

Tighter Arms

Eliminate loose, sagging skin.

Improved Shape

Achieve more defined arm contours.

Confidence Boost

Feel comfortable wearing sleeveless clothing.

Who is it for?

  • Loose or sagging upper arm skin
  • Post weight loss patients
  • Reduced skin elasticity

Procedure Options

Mini Arm Lift

For mild skin laxity with smaller incision.

Full Arm Lift

For significant excess skin with more comprehensive reshaping.

Your Journey

Consultation → Preparation → Procedure → Recovery

Common Questions

Will there be visible scars?
Scars are placed along the inner arm and fade over time.
How long is recovery?
Most patients resume light activity within 1–2 weeks.
