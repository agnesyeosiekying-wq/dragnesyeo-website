Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)

Restoring a flatter, firmer abdomen with improved contour and definition.

Common Concerns

• Loose skin after pregnancy or weight loss
• Stubborn lower abdominal bulge
• Stretch marks and skin laxity
• Weak abdominal muscles

My Approach

A tummy tuck is not just about removing excess skin. The procedure also restores abdominal wall support and improves overall contour. The goal is a natural, proportionate silhouette that complements your body.

Benefits

• Flatter and firmer abdomen
• Improved waistline contour
• Better core support
• Removal of excess skin and stretch marks

Procedure Overview

The procedure involves removal of excess skin and tightening of abdominal muscles. Scars are placed strategically to be concealed within undergarments. Recovery typically takes a few weeks, with gradual return to normal activities.

Consultation

Each treatment is tailored to your body and goals. A consultation helps determine the safest and most suitable approach for you.
