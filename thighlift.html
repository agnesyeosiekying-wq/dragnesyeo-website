What is Thighplasty?

Thighplasty, commonly known as a thigh lift, removes excess skin and fat from the inner or outer thighs while tightening underlying tissues. This procedure improves leg contour and creates smoother, more proportionate thighs— ideal for patients with sagging skin that does not respond to diet or exercise.

Key Benefits

Smoother Contour

Achieve firmer, more sculpted thighs with improved overall leg shape.

Excess Skin Removal

Remove loose, sagging skin caused by aging or significant weight loss.

Improved Comfort

Reduce friction and improve comfort in daily activities.

Who is it for?

  • Loose or sagging inner thigh skin
  • Post weight loss patients
  • Skin irritation or chafing
  • Reduced skin elasticity

Procedure Options

Inner Thigh Lift

Targets inner thigh laxity with discreet incisions hidden in the groin area.

  • Hidden scars
  • Improved inner thigh contour
  • Shorter recovery

Full Thigh Lift

Addresses extensive skin laxity along the inner thigh for more dramatic reshaping.

  • Comprehensive contouring
  • Significant skin removal
  • Long-lasting results

Outer Thigh Lift

Improves outer thigh and hip contour, often combined with lower body lift procedures.

Common Questions

Am I a good candidate?
Ideal candidates have loose or sagging inner thigh skin, good overall health, and stable body weight. A consultation will determine suitability.
How long is recovery?
Most patients return to light activities within 1–2 weeks, with gradual improvement over several weeks.
Will there be visible scarring?
Incisions are strategically placed along the inner thigh to minimize visibility. Scars typically fade over time.
Can it be combined with other procedures?
Yes, it is often combined with liposuction or other body contouring procedures for optimal results.
How long do results last?
Results are long-lasting with stable weight and healthy lifestyle habits.
