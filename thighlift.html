What is Thighplasty?

Thighplasty, commonly known as a thigh lift, removes excess skin and fat from the inner or outer thighs while tightening underlying tissues. This procedure improves leg contour and creates smoother, more proportionate thighs— ideal for patients with sagging skin that does not respond to diet or exercise.

Key Benefits

Smoother Contour

Achieve firmer, more sculpted thighs with improved overall leg shape.

Excess Skin Removal

Remove loose, sagging skin caused by aging or significant weight loss.

Improved Comfort

Reduce friction and improve comfort when walking or wearing fitted clothing.

Who is it for?

  • Loose or sagging inner thigh skin
  • Post weight loss patients
  • Skin irritation or chafing
  • Reduced skin elasticity

Procedure Options

Inner Thigh Lift

Targets inner thigh laxity with discreet incisions hidden in the groin area.

  • Hidden scars
  • Improved inner thigh contour
  • Shorter recovery

Full Thigh Lift

Addresses extensive skin laxity along the inner thigh for more dramatic reshaping.

  • Comprehensive contouring
  • Significant skin removal
  • Long-lasting results

Outer Thigh Lift

Improves outer thigh and hip contour, often combined with lower body lift procedures.

  • Improved silhouette
  • Enhanced body proportions
  • Transformative results
