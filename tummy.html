Surgical Philosophy

Tummy Tuck (Abdominoplasty)

“A toned abdomen is not just about appearance — it’s about restoring strength, balance, and confidence.”

A tummy tuck removes excess skin and tightens weakened abdominal muscles, helping restore a flatter and firmer contour after pregnancy or weight loss.

The Procedure

What to Expect

01 — Consultation
Assessment of skin and muscle condition with personalised planning.

02 — Surgery
Removal of excess skin and tightening of abdominal muscles.

03 — Recovery
Return to light activity in 1–2 weeks, full results over time.

Types of Tummy Tuck

Full Tummy Tuck

Removes excess skin and tightens muscles for significant contour improvement.

Mini Tummy Tuck

Targets lower abdomen with smaller incision for mild skin laxity.

Is This Right for You?

Suitable for You If

  • Loose skin after pregnancy
  • Abdominal muscle separation
  • Stable body weight

Important Considerations

  • Permanent scar
  • Recovery required
  • Not for weight loss

Frequently Asked Questions

Recovery time?

Typically 2–6 weeks depending on extent.

Will there be a scar?

Yes, placed low and fades over time.

Can it fix muscle separation?

Yes, abdominal muscles are repaired.

Is liposuction included?

Often combined for better contour.
